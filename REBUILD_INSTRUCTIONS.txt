Rebuild the original .gz files from the two parts (GitHub <25MB uploads)

macOS / Linux:
  cat <file>.part01 <file>.part02 > <file>
  gunzip -c <file> > <file-without-.gz>

Windows (PowerShell):
  cmd /c copy /b <file>.part01+<file>.part02 <file>
